Help & Support
Frequently Asked Questions
How does renting work? +
Browse items, choose your rental dates, pay online and we deliver to your door. Return the item at the end of your rental period.
What if the item doesn't fit? +
Use our Style Advisor to find the right size before ordering. If there's still a fit issue, contact us within 24 hours of delivery.
How do I return items? +
Our delivery partner will pick up the item on your end date. Make sure the item is packed and ready before 10 AM on the return day.
Is there a security deposit? +
A refundable security deposit is collected at the time of delivery and returned after the item is received back in good condition.
